Pasta Sauce

The best pasta sauce there is

This pasta sauce is excellent in lasagna or as a meat sauce. I use it in my popular lasagna recipe, so I thought I'd share it on its own.

Ingredients

  • 1 pund sweet italian sausage
  • 0.75 pund lean ground beef
  • 0.5 cup minced onion
  • 2 gloves garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans tomato sauce
  • 2 cans tomato paste
  • 0.5 cups of water
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1.5 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seed
  • 0.5 teaspoon salt
  • 0.25 teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook sausage, beef, onion, and garlic in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat until browned; drain fat.
  2. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Mix in sugar, basil, Italian seasoning, fennel seed, salt, and pepper.
  3. Cover and simmer, stirring occasionally, until cooked through, about 1 1/2 hours.
